#  Sales Bottleneck Analysis

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on identifying **sales bottlenecks** using transactional sales data. The analysis highlights areas where sales performance is affected due to **cancellations, returns, low regional performance, and product issues**.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze completed, cancelled, and returned orders
* Identify regions and categories with low performance
* Find products with high return rates
* Study monthly sales trends
* Support business decisions using data-driven insights

---

##  Dataset Details

**File Name:** `RegionalSales2025.csv`

### Key Columns:

* OrderID – Unique order ID
* Date – Transaction date
* CustomerID – Customer identifier
* Region – East, West, North, South
* ProductName – Product name
* Category – Product category
* Quantity – Units sold
* UnitPrice – Price per unit
* TotalAmount – Quantity × UnitPrice
* OrderStatus – Completed / Cancelled / Returned
* SalesAgent – Assigned sales agent

---

##  Tools Used

* SQL – Data analysis
* Microsoft Power BI – Dashboard & visualization
* Excel / CSV – Data source

---

##  Dashboard Visuals

* Line Chart – Monthly sales trend
* Matrix / Heatmap – Region vs Category sales
* Stacked Bar Chart – Order status by region
* KPI Cards – Total sales, average order value, cancellations, returns

---

##  Key Insights

* Revenue loss observed due to cancellations and returns
* Certain products show higher return frequency
* Sales performance varies across regions
* Few sales agents contribute major completed sales
* Monthly sales trend shows seasonal fluctuations

---

##  Conclusion

The Sales Bottleneck Analysis helps identify weak points in the sales process. By reducing cancellations, improving product quality, and optimizing regional strategies, overall business performance can be improved.
